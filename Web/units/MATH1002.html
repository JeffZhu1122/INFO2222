Mode: Normal-Day

Pre-Requisites: None.

Prohibitions: MATH1902, MATH1012, MATH1014

Brief Handbook Description: MATH1002 is designed to provide a thorough preparation for further study in mathematics and statistics. It is a core unit of study providing three of the twelve credit points required by the Faculty of Science as well as a Junior level requirement in the Faculty of Engineering.

This unit of study introduces vectors and vector algebra, linear algebra including solutions of linear systems, matrices, determinants, eigenvalues and eigenvectors.

Assumed Knowledge: HSC Mathematics or MATH1111
